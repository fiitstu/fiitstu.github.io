## Evaluation
Maximum number of points is `100`.
- `20` points for **test 1**.
- `20` points for **test 2**.
- `60` points for the final **exam**.

If you got $\le 15$ points for `2` **test**s cumulative, you can’t write the **exam**.

Points-to-Marks table is the same as [[Default FIIT STU Points-to-Marks table]].
## Miscellaneous
- Solve process is also checked in exams. So, even if you got incorrect result but the solving process is correct, you will still have some points.
- There will be no homework.
- Tests can be checked by teacher for `1` week.
- There will be no theory on **test**s (incl. definitions). So, you just have to know to use your knowledge to solve tasks.
- Calculator is not allowed neither on **test**s nor the **exam**.
- Only **Real** ($\mathbb R$) numbers will be used during the subject. You have not to deal with **Complex** numbers, for example, if you got *negative* Discriminant in a Square equation.
