## Evaluation
Maximum number of points is `100`.
- `30` points for *every-week* **task**s.
- `20` points for **test**s during the course.
- `50` points for the final **exam**.

If you got $\le 25$ points for **task**s and **test**s cumulative, you can’t write the **exam**. If you got $\le 25$ for the **exam**, you can’t complete the course successfully.
