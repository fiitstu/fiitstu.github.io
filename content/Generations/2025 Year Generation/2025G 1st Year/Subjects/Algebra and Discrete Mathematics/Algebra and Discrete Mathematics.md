## Evaluation
Maximum number of points is `100`.
- `25` points for **test 1** (on `7th` week, `5-6` problems to solve).
- `20` points for **test 2** (on `11th` week, `4` problems to solve).
- `20` points for **mini-test**s (nearly `5-7` **mini-test**s are expected).
- `35` points for the final **exam**.

If you missed at least one seminar without a reason and didn’t paid for this (see [[#Miscellaneous]]), you can’t complete the course successfully. If you got $\le 50\%$ points for `2` **test**s and **mini-test**s cumulative, you can’t write the **exam**. If you got $\le 50\%$ points for the final **exam**, you can’t complete the course successfully.

Points-to-Marks table is the same as [[Default FIIT STU Points-to-Marks table]].
## Homework
There will be homework every week. Each homework is due the next lecture.

The homework must be in `.pdf` format and is sent via [[AIS]].

*Please note that each homework needs to be confirmed twice when uploading.*
## Miscellaneous
- If you missed a seminar you can “pay” for your seminar absence. The amount is `100` solved problems in the current theme (you can even use online generators to generate the problems to solve).
