To connect your enterprise [[University Mail]] in [[STU]] to default (Apple) Mail app on iOS and other Apple OSes, follow the next steps:

1. Open (Apple) Mail's settings (Go to **Settings** -> **Apps** -> **Mail**).
2. Go to **Mail Accounts** list -> **Add Account**.
3. Among popular account providers select **Other** and then click **Add Mail Account**.
4. Enter your email (in format `x<last_name>@stuba.sk`) and your password and click **Next**.
5. In **Icoming Mail Server** section fill the following fields:
    1. **Host Name** -- `imap.stuba.sk`.
    2. **User Name** -- AIS login in format `x<last_name>`.
6. In **Outgoing Mail Server** section fill the following fields:
    1. **Host Name** -- `mail.stuba.sk`.
7. Then click **Next** and if everything went correct you have paired your STU email to Apple Mail and now you can see both personal and university mails in one place!

*Please note that [[AIS]] mail and [[University Mail]] (https://webmail.stuba.sk) are different things. We connected the last one in this guide.*
